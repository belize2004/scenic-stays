NO. 02 — QUESTIONS ASKED OFTEN

What time is check in, and can we arrive early?

Our standard arrival begins at 4:00 PM Central, while departure is requested by 10:00 AM Central. While we strive to accommodate early arrivals, they are strictly subject to our cleaning and maintenance schedule. Please inquire 24 hours prior to your stay.

Is there a minimum stay?

Minimum stays vary by season and specific collection properties, generally ranging from three to seven nights.

What is included in the house?

Every Scenic Stay is fully provisioned with premium linens, essential kitchenware, and high-speed connectivity. Specific amenity lists are available on each property's detailed portfolio page.

How do we get the keys and door codes?

Access credentials and specific entry instructions are dispatched via our encrypted guest portal 72 hours before arrival.

What happens if a hurricane is forecast?

We follow strict evacuation mandates issued by local authorities. We highly recommend travel insurance to cover weather-related disruptions.

Are pets allowed?

Specific homes in our Heritage and Coast collections are pet-friendly. Please utilize the "Pet Friendly" filter during your search.

Is there a cleaning fee, and what does it cover?

A departure cleaning fee is applied to all bookings. This covers professional sanitization and preparation for the following guest.

Do you manage homes for owners?

Yes, we provide boutique management services for a curated selection of properties. Inquire via the "Partnership" portal.
